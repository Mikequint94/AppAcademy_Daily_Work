Steamed Water Spinach

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe to make a healthy dish!

Click here for Recipe

Ingredients

  • Water spinach
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Fermented tofu
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce

Cooking directions

  1. Cut water spinach into 3-4 inch segments
  2. Wash, drain, and set water spinach aside
  3. Fry garlic in oil
  4. Add ginger
  5. Add water spinach and sauté
  6. Mix in fermented tofu
  7. Add a splash of soy sauce
